Errant Priest - Global Game Jam (2016)

Errant Priest is a 2D side scrolling adventure game. The player gets "Potential Mana" from killing enemies and does not get to use it until the player performs a ritual at any shrine. The player then gets to chose which spells they wants to bring to the field to combat their enemies.

Hammerfirst (2015)

A game where First-Person Brawling meets Boxercising. Developed in Unity using a Myo Armband as the controller. The player takes control of a descendant of a god to fight creatures that have escaped from their prison. I worked on core gameplay mechanics, such as Punching and Blocking.

Spooky Spelunky - Global Game Jam (2015)

You and another player take control of two miners who have just survived a cave in and must now navigate your way through the labyrinth of mineshafts and fend off horrible creatures. One player takes the responsibility of fighting while the other must hold up the flash light to illuminate the pitch black caves.
